Our Story

Brewed with
Love Since 2025

Tara Kape started with a simple dream — to create a coffee shop where Calambenos could gather, relax, and enjoy great coffee without pretension. Our name says it all: "Tara, kape!" — Come, let's have coffee.

We partner with small-scale Filipino coffee farmers and bakers to bring you authentic, locally crafted flavors. Every cup you enjoy supports a Filipino livelihood.
